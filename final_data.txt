Notes:
The final project includes additional functionality compared to previous laboratory exercises, including GPS communication, geofence processing, multiple FreeRTOS tasks, queues, event flags, timers, buzzer circuitry, and GPS power-control hardware, resulting in higher active current consumption.

==================================================
KNOWN LIMITATIONS
==================================================

- GPS accuracy indoors is limited and very small geofence radii may produce false geofence breaches due to normal GPS position drift.
- Occasionally invalid GPS coordinates may be received during GPS acquisition and are ignored by the application logic.
- GPS cold-start acquisition time depends on satellite visibility and may take significantly longer than warm-start acquisition.
